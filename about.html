About Us

We are pleased to introduce to you our firm. the Company which is solely indigenous and registered with the Corporate affairs Commission with Certificate NO.928470 of 31st july, 1995. Our sole objective is to render specialized and professional service in the area of Aluminum fabrication and installation to a diverse range of clients. Our experience covers the entire Spectrum Of Fabrication,Installation and Maintenance of:

  1. Aluminum Doors
  2. Aluminum Windows
  3. Office partitioning
  4. Suspended Ceilings
  5. Aluminum Roofing
  6. Window Blinds
  7. Steel Works
In the past Ten years, we have handled projects and specialist Aluminum Works for different organizations. Over the years, we have grown a list of reputable entities and individual in our client profile, this includes among others the following:
  1. Partitioning/Curtain walling, cashier cubicle and window/door for the new Zenith Bank Plc, Ejigbo Branch.(Sub-contractor by San's Frontiers Ltd).
  2. Internal Aluminum Partitioning, Window and Door for the recently Commissioned Zenith Banks Plc, Along Ozumba Mbadiwe Road.(Sub-contracted by Marble Concept).
  3. Partitioning of cashier's cubicles for Cash Adoption, Zenith Bank Plc, Airport road 1, Ogundu branch and Ikorodu branch, Allen branch.
